DRISHTI LIBRARY

A hidlo of a library for manipulating PNG, JPEG, TIFF images.Started January 20, 2020 [Round#4]

Why another imaging library

This machine vision library, supports manipulation and generation of PNG, JPEG, TIFF type images. We have added static library for various interface classes [see docs] which can be used by either calling calc() / draw() functions. Our dependency free library is a core imaging library at Radii.

Why Now?

This power to see witness and hence manipulate and decide adaptations for your business, either automatic or manual has been a long drawn endeavor for human machine partnership. We aim to strengthen the correlation by correcting the mechanics around it. These days, it's unusual to experience an electronic consumer product that doesn't use EYES for Augmentation to create enhanced decisons for its users. Sensors have experienced a renaissance of sorts as micro-electromechanical systems (MEMS) technology becomes less expensive and further miniaturized, in turn fueling penetration of sensors, eyes' collaboration into new applications and creating new potential for the sensor market.
